Take Friction Further

The experiments in this book are a starting point. For leaders and organizations ready to go deeper, Leading Through Friction™ is a leadership development program from TalentSmartEQ, based on the research and framework introduced in these pages.

Friction shows up in every organization where people care about the work — the hard conversation you’re avoiding, the tension in the room no one is naming, the pressure and uncertainty that comes with leading a team. It’s not a sign that something is wrong. It’s a sign that something important is happening.

Most leaders are taught to eliminate friction. The best leaders learn how to work with it.

The Leading Through Friction training experience gives leaders and their teams the skills to recognize what friction is telling them and respond with intention. Leaders who go through the program are better equipped to:

•  Spot friction early, before it escalates and impacts performance

•  Respond with intention rather than react on instinct

•  Navigate tension, conflict, and ambiguity more effectively

•  Build the trust and alignment that drives team performance
