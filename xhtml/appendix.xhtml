Appendix SURVEY METHODOLOGY AND DEMOGRAPHICS

Survey Methodology

The data presented in this book draws from the National Emotions Study, a survey on emotional intelligence and workplace emotion among U.S. adults, conducted by TalentSmartEQ in 2025. The survey was fielded online by Qualtrics between October 2025 and January 2026, with a final sample of 1,188 respondents. Qualtrics recruited participants from its online research panel using demographic quotas designed to produce a nationally representative sample of U.S. adults, matched to U.S. Census benchmarks on age, gender, race/ethnicity, and region.

The tables below provide a demographic breakdown of survey respondents, shown overall and split between non-leaders and leaders.
